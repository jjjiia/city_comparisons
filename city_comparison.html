This map compares median household income between cities.
Inspired by cost of living calculators online, this map gets census block group level data about income, housing, and transportation. It works like a calculator by translating between city areas rather than dollar units, choose your income on the scroll bar and it will show the areas in other cities you can live in and the demographics and costs of those areas.
